## **1️⃣ Game Overview**

**Title:** Fight or Flight  
 **Genre:** Action / Adventure / Flying Platformer  
 **Target Age:** 6–12 (Family-friendly superhero theme)  
 **Platform:** PC, Web, or Android (via GameMaker.io or Unity/Maximo workflow)  
 **Game Loop:** Fly → Collect → Power Up → Face Villain → Retreat or Win → Unlock next stage

**Core Theme:**  
 Courage isn’t about fighting — it’s about knowing when to fly, learn, and come back stronger.

---

## **2️⃣ Story & World**

**Setting:**  
 A bright, comic-inspired sky world that blends futuristic cities, mystical clouds, and elemental realms (storm, lightning, gravity, etc.).

**Hero Origin:**  
 A young flyer discovers they have powers that react to emotion — courage fuels “Fight,” fear fuels “Flight.” Mentor guides them through training until villains begin disturbing the skies.

**Villains (Boss Order):**

1. Tempestra – Mistress of Storms 🌩️ *(Levels 1–3)*

2. Volt Vortex – Electric manipulator ⚡ *(Levels 4–6)*

3. Mirage – Illusion and deception 🌀 *(Levels 7–9)*

4. Gravitor – Gravity distortion 🌌 *(Levels 10–12)*

5. The Shadow King – Fear itself 🕶️ *(Final Levels)*

Each villain introduces a new gameplay twist (wind, lightning, mirror doubles, zero gravity, darkness).

---

## **3️⃣ Gameplay Mechanics**

| Mechanic | Description | Purpose |
| ----- | ----- | ----- |
| **Flight Control** | Tap/hold to fly higher; glide to descend | Smooth, kid-friendly motion |
| **Collectibles (Power Orbs)** | Gather energy to fill Super Meter | Core reward loop |
| **Obstacles** | Clouds, drones, lightning, wind gusts | Skill-building and timing |
| **Super Mode (Fight)** | Temporary burst of strength (energy dash or power shot) | Adds excitement |
| **Boss Battles** | Learn and dodge attack patterns | Teaches resilience and pattern memory |
| **Retreat System (Flight)** | Hero never dies — they retreat to regroup | Keeps tone positive and empowering |
