# Company Research Prompt
Task: Research the following company and provide a concise report for David Loake (PM Candidate).

## Company:
{{company_name}}

## Research Dimensions:
1. Product Maturity: Stage (Seed/Series A/B/C/Public), age, market position.
2. Mission & Culture: Stated values vs. glassdoor/public sentiment.
3. Domain Complexity: Financial/Regulatory/Operational risk level.
4. Recent News: Funding rounds, product launches, layoffs, major hires.
5. Technical Stacks: Known infrastructure/platforms.
6. Target Customer: Enterprise, SME, Consumer.
7. Competitors: Who are their main rivals?
8. Hiring Context: Why are they hiring for this role now? (Growth, churn, etc.)

## Output:
- Concise markdown report.
- Risk/Opportunity assessment for a Senior/Lead PM.
- "Why David?" pitch based on company research.
