Delete Account or Data

Sprocket account deletion, data deletion, and retention

What this page covers

This page explains how to clear data inside the Sprocket app, how to request deletion of your account and associated server-held data, what is removed, what we may retain for a limited time, and how Google Play purchases relate to account deletion.

Clear your data in the app

You can permanently remove notes, reminders, and other local app data from your device:

Step 1

Open the Sprocket app.

Step 2

Go to Settings.

Step 3

Choose Clear My Data and confirm. This removes local content and signed-in backups of notes and reminders as described in the privacy policy.

Request account or server data deletion

If you signed in with Google (or another method the app supports) and want your account and associated server-held data deleted, email us from the address linked to your account where possible.

Email request

Send to help@arcturusdc.com with the subject line Sprocket data deletion request and describe what you need removed (for example: full account deletion, or specific data categories).

We may need to verify account ownership before processing a deletion request.

What is typically deleted

  • Your Sprocket account record (where an account exists)
  • Backed-up notes and reminders tied to that account
  • Associated usage or entitlement records we can unlink after verification, subject to fraud-prevention and legal requirements

Retention period

Deletion window

Verified account and associated data deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

Selective data-deletion requests are also handled within 30 days of verification where feasible.

Aggregated or anonymised analytics that cannot be linked back to you may be retained.

Important notes

  • Deleting your Sprocket account or data does not automatically cancel or refund a Google Play purchase. Manage purchases and refunds in your Google Play account.
  • If you use subscriptions or entitlements managed via Google Play or third-party tooling (for example RevenueCat), removal of account data may affect restore behaviour; contact us if you need help after deletion.
