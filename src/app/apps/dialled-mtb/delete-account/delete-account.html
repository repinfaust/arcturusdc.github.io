Delete Account

Dialled MTB account deletion instructions and retention details

What this page covers

This page explains how a Dialled MTB user can request account deletion, what data is deleted, what may be retained temporarily, and what to do about any active Premium subscription.

How to delete your account

Dialled MTB account deletion is handled inside the app.

Step 1

Open the Dialled MTB app and sign in to the account you want to delete.

Step 2

Go to Settings.

Step 3

Select Delete Account and confirm the deletion request.

What is deleted

  • Your Dialled MTB account record
  • Your saved bike profiles
  • Your ride records stored in Dialled MTB
  • Your maintenance history, service records, and notes

Retention period

Deletion window

Account data is permanently deleted within 30 days of the deletion request.

Anonymised analytics data cannot be linked back to a deleted account.

Important notes

  • Deleting your Dialled MTB account does not automatically cancel an Apple App Store or Google Play subscription.
  • If you have an active Premium subscription, cancel it separately in your Apple or Google subscription settings.
  • If you connected Strava, you can also revoke Dialled MTB access from your Strava account settings.
