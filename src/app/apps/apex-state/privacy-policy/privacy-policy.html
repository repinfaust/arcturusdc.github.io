Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28 May 2026  ·  Apex State iOS & Android

The short version

Apex State collects only what it needs to provide your track-day setup and session features. Your setup data is private by default. AI assistant queries are processed by OpenAI. Authentication is handled by Apple or Google — we never see your payment details. We do not sell your data.

1. Who We Are

Apex State (“the App”, “we”, “our”, “us”) is operated by ARCTURUS DIGITAL CONSULTING LIMITED.

Email: info@arcturusdc.com

2. What Apex State Does

Apex State is a motorcycle track setup and riding companion application that allows riders to:

  • Log bike setup data
  • Record track sessions
  • Store tyre pressures and suspension settings
  • Compare previous setups
  • Share limited anonymised paddock data
  • Use AI-powered setup assistance features

3. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Apple or Google Sign-In, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifier
  • Profile image (if provided by your provider)

Authentication is handled securely through Firebase Authentication.

Rider & Setup Data

You may voluntarily provide:

  • Bike information
  • Track session details
  • Tyre pressures
  • Suspension settings
  • Electronics settings
  • Notes and riding feedback
  • Lap times and performance information

Technical & Usage Data

We may collect:

  • Device type
  • App version
  • Crash diagnostics
  • Basic analytics events
  • Authentication activity

AI Assistant Interactions

If you use AI-powered assistant features, the prompts and related context you submit may be securely processed by OpenAI APIs in order to generate responses.

Do not submit sensitive personal, medical, financial, or confidential information into AI prompts.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App
  • Store and sync your riding and setup data
  • Authenticate users
  • Improve features and reliability
  • Provide AI-generated setup assistance
  • Support paddock comparison features
  • Diagnose bugs and crashes
  • Improve user experience

5. Paddock Sharing & Visibility

Certain setup and session data may be visible to other authenticated users through community or paddock-style comparison features. This may include:

  • Bike make and model
  • Track name
  • Tyre compounds
  • Tyre pressures
  • Lap times
  • Limited setup information

We do not intentionally expose your email address or private account information to other users. Future versions of the App may include additional privacy controls and opt-out settings.

Some anonymised analytics or diagnostic information may remain in backups or aggregated systems for a limited period.
