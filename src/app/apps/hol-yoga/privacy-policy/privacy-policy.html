Privacy

Privacy Policy & Data Removal

Effective date: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Heart of Living Yoga mobile app ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. The app is provided by Arcturus Digital Consulting Ltd on behalf of Heart of Living Yoga (registered charity no. 1169252).

Heart of Living Yoga is currently in development. This policy describes how the app is designed to handle data at release. If practices change before or after launch, this page will be updated with a new effective date.

What the app is

Heart of Living Yoga is a heart-centred practice app offering guided practices, live daily meditations, yoga class videos, a music library, information about retreats and events, and ways to support the charity.

Information we collect

Account information

If you create an account or sign in, we collect the details needed to authenticate you — typically your email address and a display name. Authentication is handled by Google Firebase. We do not see or store your password.

Usage of content

To provide core features, the app may record which practices, classes, or tracks you have opened or marked as favourites, and your session preferences, so your library reflects your use. This is limited to what the app needs to function.

Supporter and payment information

If you choose a deluxe supporter option or make a donation, the payment is processed by the relevant app store (Apple App Store or Google Play) or a payment provider. We do not receive or store your full card details. We may receive confirmation that a purchase or supporter status is active.

Technical and diagnostic information

We may collect basic technical information such as app version, device type, and operating system to keep the app working and to diagnose problems. Live meditations and video/music content may be delivered through third-party services (for example a streaming or video provider), which receive the technical information necessary to deliver that content.

The app does not sell your personal information, and does not use it for third-party advertising.

How we use information

  • To sign you in and keep your account secure.
  • To provide practices, live meditations, videos, and music.
  • To remember your preferences and library.
  • To process supporter memberships and donations.
  • To keep the app reliable and fix problems.
  • To communicate with you about the app or your account where necessary.

Service providers

We use trusted third-party services to run the app. These may include:

  • Google Firebase — authentication and app data.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution and in-app purchases.
  • Video, music, and live-session providers — to deliver classes, tracks, and live meditations.

Each provider processes data under its own privacy terms and only to provide its service to us.

Data retention

We keep account and usage information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove or anonymise the associated personal data as described below, except where we are required to keep limited records (for example, transaction records for financial or legal compliance).

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise these rights using the contact details below.

Deleting your account and data

You can request deletion of your Heart of Living Yoga account and associated personal data at any time.

In the app

Once released, open Settings and choose Delete account. This removes your account and associated personal data.

By email

Email help@arcturusdc.com from the address linked to your account with the subject line Delete my Heart of Living Yoga data. We will verify your request and delete your data.

We aim to complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. Some limited information may be retained where required by law (for example, records of financial transactions), and anonymised or aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained.

Children

The app is intended for a general adult audience. It is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact help@arcturusdc.com.
