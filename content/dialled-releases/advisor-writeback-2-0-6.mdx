The Advisor can now propose concrete setup values alongside its advice —
fork and shock pressure, sag, tyre pressures. Review the proposal, tick the
fields you want, and apply them to your bike in one tap. Applied values are
marked "Suggested by Advisor" until you confirm them yourself, so a
suggestion never silently becomes ground truth.
